About Tash Auto Consults

Welcome to Tash Auto Consults, your trusted partner in automobile sales and consultancy services in Kenya. We specialize in vehicle sourcing—offering both new, foreign-used and Kenyan-used cars—along with seamless vehicle importation solutions tailored to your needs. Our expert team provides pre-purchase inspections to ensure quality and reliability, along with personalized consultancy to help you choose the right vehicle based on your budget, preferences, and requirements.
Additionally, we guide clients on spare parts sourcing and proper vehicle maintenance for long-term performance. At Tash Auto Consults, we are committed to delivering transparent, efficient, and customer-focused automotive solutions. Let us drive your journey to the perfect car!
