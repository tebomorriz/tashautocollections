Pre-Purchase Inspection

Protect yourself from costly surprises by letting our experts inspect the vehicle before you buy. We offer thorough checks to ensure you're making a safe investment.

What We Check

Engine & Transmission

We run diagnostics and check for leaks, wear, overheating, and transmission performance issues.

Exterior & Frame

Full check of body condition, signs of accidents, paintwork, lights, and structural integrity.

Interior & Electronics

We inspect air conditioning, dashboard electronics, seats, infotainment system, and safety features.

Why Pre-Purchase Inspection is Essential

1. Avoid Hidden Issues

Many problems aren’t visible to the untrained eye. Our professionals catch what sellers often overlook or hide.

2. Buy with Confidence

With our inspection report, you’ll know exactly what you’re buying — and can negotiate or walk away with peace of mind.
